Church of Shar

Welcome to the Sanctuary of Shadows

About Us

The Church of Shar is the congregation of those who seek the comfort of shadows and the wisdom of the night. We are followers of Shar, the Mistress of the Night, guiding souls through darkness towards enlightenment.

Our Beliefs

  • Mystery and Night: We embrace the mysteries of the world and find solace in the night.
  • Secrecy and Loss: We value the power of secrets and understand the profound growth that comes with loss.
  • Strength in Darkness: We believe in the strength that comes from understanding and embracing one's own darkness.

Services and Rituals

  • Nightly Vigils: Join us every evening as we gather to honor the Lady of Loss through quiet reflection and meditation.
  • New Moon Festivities: Celebrate the darkness of the new moon with us in a night of silent contemplation and secret revelations.
  • Counsel of Shadows: Seek guidance from our clergy who can help you find your path through the darkness.

Community Outreach

  • Shadow Workshops: Engage in workshops designed to help you explore your inner darkness and learn how to use it as a source of strength.
  • Benevolence of the Night: Participate in our efforts to provide aid to those who have been touched by loss and seek the comfort of the shadows.

Join Us

  • Membership: Feel the call of the dark? Reach out to us for more information on how to become a part of our congregation.
  • Contact: Send us a message through the veil of night, and a member of our clergy will respond to your inquiry.
